Popular Movies

"Joker" is a 2019 psychological thriller film directed by Todd Phillips.The film delves into Arthur's troubled life as he grapples with societal rejection, poverty, and a lack of empathy from those around him. As he faces numerous setbacks and humiliation, Arthur's fragile sanity unravels, leading him on a path of violence and anarchy.

"Doraemon: Stand by Me" is a 2014 animated adventure film based on the popular Japanese manga and anime series, Doraemon. The story revolves around a young boy named Nobita Nobi and his robot cat friend, Doraemon, who travels back in time from the future to help Nobita overcome his present-day challenges.

"Interstellar" is a 2014 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film focuses on Cooper, a former NASA pilot played by Matthew McConaughey, who is chosen to lead the expedition. As they traverse the vastness of space, the crew encounters mind-bending phenomena and grapples with the complexities of time dilation. "Interstellar" explores profound questions about the nature of time and the limits of human knowledge.

Other Movies

"John Wick" is a thrilling action film franchise that follows the story of the titular character, played by Keanu Reeves. John Wick is a retired hitman who is reluctantly drawn back into the dangerous world of assassins after the tragic loss of his wife and the theft of his beloved car, unleashing a relentless pursuit of vengeance.

"Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle" is an adventure-comedy film that serves as a standalone sequel to the 1995 movie "Jumanji." The story revolves around a group of four high school students who discover an old video game console while serving detention. As they choose their avatars and begin playing, they are magically transported into the game's virtual jungle world.

The Avengers film series brings together a diverse group of superheroes from the Marvel Comics universe to save the world from formidable threats. The core team includes iconic characters such as Iron Man (Tony Stark), Captain America (Steve Rogers), Thor, Hulk (Bruce Banner), Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff), and Hawkeye (Clint Barton).

"War" is an action-packed thriller film that explores the intense rivalry between two highly skilled agents, Kabir (played by Hrithik Roshan) and Khalid (played by Tiger Shroff). Kabir, a former mentor to Khalid, has gone rogue and becomes a dangerous enemy. As the two agents engage in a deadly cat-and-mouse game across international locations.

"Star Wars" is an iconic space opera film franchise set in a galaxy far, far away. The saga follows the eternal struggle between the forces of good, represented by the Jedi knights, and the dark side, led by the Sith lords. The story primarily focuses on the Skywalker family, starting with Anakin Skywalker's journey from being a promising Jedi to his transformation into the fearsome Sith Lord Darth Vader.

"MOM" is an Indian thriller film that tells the story of Devki, played by Sridevi, a loving and protective stepmother. Devki's relationship with her stepdaughter Arya becomes strained after an unfortunate incident occurs, leading Arya to distance herself from Devki. However, when Arya falls victim to a heinous crime, Devki sets out on a quest for justice, taking matters into her own hands.

"Police Story 2013" is an action-packed crime thriller directed by Ding Sheng and starring Jackie Chan. The film follows the story of a dedicated police officer named Zhong Wen, who is called to a luxurious nightclub by his estranged daughter Miao Miao. Once there, Zhong Wen discovers that the club has been taken over by a group of dangerous criminals.

"2012" is an epic disaster film directed by Roland Emmerich. The story centers around the apocalyptic predictions surrounding the end of the Mayan calendar in 2012. As the Earth faces a series of catastrophic natural disasters, including massive earthquakes, tsunamis, and volcanic eruptions.

Circus
Fire Eating

Experience the mesmerizing spectacle of fire-eating at our park, where skilled performers dazzle you with their daring acts. Watch in awe as they manipulate flames with precision and grace, creating a breathtaking display of artistry and danger.

Tightrope Walking

Witness the incredible art of tightrope walking at our park, where fearless performers defy gravity and test their balance on a thin wire high above the ground. Marvel at their astonishing skills as they navigate the tightrope with grace and precision, captivating the audience with each step. Get ready for an awe-inspiring display of bravery and agility that will leave you on the edge of your seat.

Aerial Acrobatics

Watch in awe as talented artists take to the skies, showcasing their aerial acrobatics and mesmerizing maneuvers. From stunning aerial silk routines to gravity-defying trapeze acts, our aerial performers will leave you spellbound with their grace, strength, and daring stunts. Experience the thrill and beauty of these captivating performances that will transport you to new heights of excitement and wonder.

Karaoke

Unleash your inner superstar and take center stage at our park's exhilarating karaoke sessions. Sing your heart out to your favorite tunes and be the star of the show. Whether you're a seasoned performer or a first-time singer, our karaoke experience is the ultimate crowd-pleaser. Join the lively atmosphere, cheer on fellow singers, and let the music ignite your spirit of fun and entertainment. Get ready to create unforgettable memories as you belt out your favorite songs and enjoy the spotlight at one of the most popular activities in our park. It's your time to shine and make the stage your own!
