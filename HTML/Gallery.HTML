Living The Moment

We Capture the Happy Moments of Your Life That Your Spend Here!

Check out this adorable fairy in our park, spreading her magic and stealing hearts with her enchanting outfit. She's a true delight, adding a touch of whimsy and wonder to the atmosphere. Get ready to be charmed by her playful and captivating presence.

Our food zone is a hub of diversity, offering a culinary adventure where visitors can explore global flavors without leaving the park. It is a place where the aroma of spices, the sizzle of grills, and the joy of shared meals intertwine, creating an unforgettable experience that leaves taste buds tingling and hearts content.

People of all ages and backgrounds gather at communal tables or find a good spot on the grass, eagerly waiting to buy their food from food zone. Friends laugh and share bites, discovering new flavors and discussing their favorites. Families gather around picnic tables, creating lasting memories as they indulge in a feast together.

Visitors of all ages go on an enchanting journey through the wonders of the underwater realm in our Aquarium. As you enter, a sense of awe washes over you, accompanied by the gentle melody of water and the mesmerizing glow of aquatic displays

Interactive exhibits engage the curious minds of visitors, allowing them to touch gentle reptiles, pet friendly farm animals, or even witness the miracle of new life at the nursery section. The zoo becomes a living classroom, inspiring a sense of respect and conservation for the creatures that inhabit our planet

The aquarium in our park is a gateway to an amazing world hidden beneath the waves. It is a place of discovery, where visitors connect with the beauty and diversity of marine life, leaving with memories of an unforgettable and enlightening experience.

The roller coasters in our park are a testament to human ingenuity and the pursuit of excitement. They provide an escape from everyday life, offering a thrilling and unforgettable experience that leaves hearts racing and faces beaming with exhilaration.

The sound of clattering wheels and screams of delight fill the air as roller coaster cars speed along the tracks. Riders brace themselves for the rush of wind against their faces and the stomach-flipping sensation as they ascend steep inclines. Nervous energy turns into pure exhilaration as the coaster hurtles down, twisting and turning in a thrilling display of gravity-defying maneuvers.

Families gather, clutching onto their safety harnesses, with eyes filled with a mix of excitement and trepidation. Children bounce with anticipation, their laughter echoing through the air, while parents share encouraging smiles and reassuring words. Friends join forces, ready to conquer the twists, turns, and drops that await them.

Families wander hand in hand, their eyes wide with anticipation as they explore the diverse exhibits. Children point in awe at the magnificent elephants, playful monkeys swinging from tree branches, and majestic big cats prowling gracefully within their enclosures.

In our park, a vibrant food scene unfolds as visitors flock to the diverse array of food vendors, creating a delightful tapestry of flavors and aromas. The air is filled with the enticing scents of freshly baked goods, sizzling grill stations, and the alluring fragrances of international cuisines.

In our park, an extraordinary opportunity awaits as visitors gather to interact with one of nature's most captivating creatures: the crocodile. With caution and guidance from trained professionals, brave individuals have the chance to touch and feel the rugged skin of these majestic reptiles.
